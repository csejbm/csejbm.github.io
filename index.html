About

I was born and raised in Southern California with a love of all the natural attractions my home has to offer. I attended the University of California, San Diego where I received a B.S. in Cognitive Science. The Cognitive Science program is an interdisciplinary field of study which allowed me to take courses in Psychology, Neuroscience, Computer Science, Linguistics, and Philosophy.

While in school I took a keen interest in the Distributed Cognition framework and the role that interactions with language, technology, art, and society play in shaping the mind and its processes. I believe that by approaching challenges from a user-centered perspective we can design solutions that extend the realm of human ability and give people more time to engage in the world around them.

Property Asset Manager

The Asset Manager product provides property leasing and sales professionals powerful insight into their own data, exposing patterns that would have previously been hidden in different spreadsheets and accounting systems. The user can visualize their data from different views and filter and sort their sales and rent data alongside property metadata to render maps, charts, site plan heat-maps, and tables that help analyze the performance of their real estate assets.

JLL Tourpoint

The JLL Tourpoint app allows users to present property site tours in an iOS optimized format. Site tours are ways that leasing professionals propose new sites to their tenants, and involve the aggregation of many data points in a visual format. Traditional site tours can take days for a marketing professional to produce, but Tourpoint site tours can easily be created by administrators online in a matter of minutes to be distributed for viewing on multiple devices. Tourpoint digital site tours are also interactive, collaborative, and always up to date.
